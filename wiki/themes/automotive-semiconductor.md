## Narrative
IrrationalAnalysis called the Mobileye collapse first and most sharply: the January 2024 pre-announcement exposed both a severe inventory hangover at Tier 1 customers and CFO-level accounting manipulation, leading to an immediate short thesis on MBLY while peers ON, STM, and NXPI were graded merely "flu-level" sick. By August 2024 the MBLY bear case hardened from inventory-driven to structural — Qualcomm's automotive SoC was identified as a superior product winning durable share, a verdict that still stood at the November 2024 earnings review. The theme then went quiet on ADAS specifically before re-emerging in 2026 through a different lens: Citrini and Semi Doped separately identified that the capex built for EV and solar power semiconductors — the same analog and power names (TXN, NXPI, discrete MOSFETs, MLCCs) that suffered the post-EV bust — is exactly the infrastructure AI data centers are now inheriting at 800V rack architectures, converting stranded automotive supply chain investment into a secular AI power demand story. The theme has thus evolved from a cyclical short on ADAS SoC inventory in early 2024 to a more constructive, maturing view on analog and power semiconductor suppliers whose automotive-era capex now positions them for AI-driven ASP expansion.
