## Narrative
IrrationalAnalysis introduced the power semiconductors theme in late 2024 by flagging Wolfspeed alongside broader semi coverage and then dedicating a full piece to switching regulators and VRMs — establishing the link between wide bandgap materials (SiC, GaN) and AI datacenter power delivery. Coverage stayed sparse through mid-2025, with only a Navitas note and an Aehr test equipment mention, but exploded in April-May 2026 as the 800V datacenter architecture debate crystallized: IrrationalAnalysis published dense multi-angle coverage on GaN at 800V, SiC vs. GaN tradeoffs, and Wolfspeed's 10 kV ambitions, while SemiAnalysis delivered a landmark piece on the 800 VDC datacenter revolution and SemiDoped framed power delivery as the next physics wall for AI scaling. Citrini joined in May 2026 with a supply-chain inheritance angle, signaling the theme was crossing from specialist to generalist investor awareness. As of late May 2026, the theme is maturing rapidly: the technical debate has shifted from "will wide bandgap matter?" to "which voltage architecture wins and which device makers capture margin," with Wolfspeed, Navitas, and the broader SiC/GaN supply chain under active scrutiny across at least four publications.
