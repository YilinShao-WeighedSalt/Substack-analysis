## Narrative
This theme emerged in late 2024 with irrationalanalysis framing semiconductor equity picks and M&A opinions (Ampere valuation, Qualcomm-Intel) in explicitly investor-facing terms, blending sector analysis with portfolio rationale. Through 2025, both irrationalanalysis and semianalysis broadened this posture: portfolio updates, quarterly favorite-names lists, and cost-of-AI infrastructure pieces all foregrounded the reader's financial stake rather than pure engineering analysis. The April 2026 cluster around "it's just money, made up" marks a philosophical inflection — irrationalanalysis turning the lens on market psychology and macro uncertainty — while semianalysis's May 2026 piece on AI output costs continues the tradition of translating deep technical work into capital-allocation terms. The theme is maturing: multiple publications have now adopted a semi-explicit personal-finance register, and the cadence of portfolio-update and cost-framing pieces has increased through 2025-2026.
