## Thesis evolution
As of September 2024, irrationalanalysis initiated a long position in FSLR based on First Solar's differentiated cadmium telluride (CdTe) thin-film technology, which offers better degradation characteristics over a panel's lifetime compared to conventional silicon-based competitors — a meaningful advantage in utility-scale projects where long-term energy yield directly affects project economics. The publication acknowledged policy risk (IRA tailwinds could be curtailed), but viewed First Solar's domestic manufacturing footprint as a structural moat that would persist across policy regimes. No other publications in the related set have weighed in on FSLR, so there is no inter-publication disagreement to adjudicate at this time.
