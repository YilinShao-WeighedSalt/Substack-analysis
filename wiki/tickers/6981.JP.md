## Thesis evolution
Citrini introduced Murata as a prospective long in July 2024 as part of a broad "picks and shovels" basket for on-device AI inference, grouping it with TDK (6762.JP) and Taiyo Yuden (6976.JP) as Japanese RF and passive component suppliers positioned to benefit from the AI smartphone upgrade cycle. The core logic is that next-generation AI iPhones — beginning with the iPhone 16/17 — will require more sophisticated RF front-end modules to support the increased connectivity demands of on-device inference and Apple Intelligence features. No subsequent publications in the wiki have updated or contested this view, leaving the thesis as a single-source, single-call initiation with medium conviction implied by Citrini's "high on list" framing rather than an outright owned position.

## Outcome tracking
No price at call was recorded for 6981.JP, so quantitative outcome assessment is not possible.
