## Thesis evolution
[[irrationalanalysis]] introduced TTMI in December 2025 as a single-sentence idea in the "Miscellaneous Longs" section of the annual Irrational Ideas post, citing its position as one of the rare non-Taiwan manufacturers of high-end PCBs at a time of supply shortage. The call is brief and low-elaboration — no financial detail, no competitive breakdown, no price target — placing it firmly in the high-optionality, low-conviction bucket relative to the post's deeper ideas such as LITE or INTC. No other publication has weighed in, and there is no counter-thesis on record. The view is effectively a supply-constraint scarcity trade on geopolitical diversification away from Taiwan-centric PCB capacity.

## Outcome tracking
No px@call value was recorded at the time of the call, so quantitative assessment is not possible.
