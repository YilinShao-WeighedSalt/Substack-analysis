## Thesis evolution
SWKS entered the record in July 2024 as a low-conviction basket mention from Citrini — an iPhone RF supplier acknowledged as basket-eligible but ranked below QRVO in the on-device inference supply chain. Irrationalanalysis built a bearish case through early 2025: Skyworks had lost Apple RF front-end share to Broadcom, whose superior filters were compensating for the performance gap in Apple's internally developed C1 modem, and the broader RFFE category was labeled "radioactive." The iPhone 16e teardown in March 2025 confirmed the damage — Qorvo had taken additional share from Skyworks within the handset itself. The view reversed tactically one week later when irrationalanalysis proposed a macro-neutral pair trade: long SWKS (alongside CEVA, SITM, and QRVO) against short QCOM, on the thesis that Apple's aggressive internal modem ramp would devastate Qualcomm's revenue base while the remaining RFFE suppliers would be relative beneficiaries of the content reshuffling. The two publications are not in substantive disagreement so much as operating at different levels of detail — Citrini never revisited SWKS after the initial mention, while irrationalanalysis moved from short to a hedged long within a single month, driven by the modem ramp trade logic rather than any change in SWKS's standalone competitive position.
