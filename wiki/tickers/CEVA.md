## Thesis evolution
The bull case on CEVA was initiated in February 2025 by irrationalanalysis as a direct corollary to the Apple C1 modem thesis: CEVA earns a royalty (estimated 0.5%–1%) on every Apple cellular and Wi-Fi+BT chip that incorporates its DSP IP, and the street was seen as badly undermodeling the pace of Apple's modem ramp across the full iPhone lineup. The March 2025 follow-up framed CEVA as a leg in a macro-neutral spread — long Apple modem ecosystem names (CEVA, SITM, QRVO, SWKS) against a short in QCOM — exploiting the asymmetry that QCOM has no offsetting revenue while CEVA's royalty stream grows with every iPhone unit shifted to the internal modem. The May 2025 note reinforced conviction after Qualcomm's own earnings call disclosed that ~30% of September 2025 iPhones (base and Air, not just Air as prior consensus assumed) would use the C1 modem — a faster displacement than expected. Irrationalanalysis has been consistently LONG across all three appearances with no dissenting view; the thesis has only strengthened as the Apple modem timeline accelerated.

## Outcome tracking
No px@call values are available for any of the three CEVA entries, so direct price-based assessment is not possible. The thesis trajectory, however, has tracked favorably: each successive note brought a concrete positive catalyst (the macro-neutral trade framing in March, the confirmed accelerated ramp in May), and the author's stated desire to add shares in May 2025 while being margin-constrained implies the position was not yet fully sized — suggesting further upside was anticipated. The live view would be falsified if Apple's internal modem program stalls (e.g., mmWave certification failures delaying full displacement beyond 2028), if CEVA loses its DSP IP socket in a future Apple modem generation, or if royalty rates are renegotiated materially downward.
