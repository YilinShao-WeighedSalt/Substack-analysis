## Thesis evolution
The bearish view on ABB originates from SemiAnalysis's March 2025 analysis of the robotics labor economy, which identifies ABB as one of the traditional Big Four incumbents (alongside FANUC, Yaskawa, and KUKA) facing structural disruption. The concern is that ABB has underinvested in R&D relative to emerging Chinese competitors such as ESTUN, Inovance, and others that are rapidly closing the technology gap. As Chinese robotics firms approach 50% domestic market share and begin exporting, ABB's pricing power and volume in its core markets are seen as increasingly at risk. No bullish counterpoint from other tracked publications exists in the record at this time.
