## Thesis evolution
The single published view, from irrationalanalysis in July 2024, frames SIMO as a leveraged-but-asset-light way to benefit from AI training storage demand: video model training is unusually NVMe-intensive, and SIMO as the dominant merchant NVMe controller vendor captures that spend without the capital intensity or pricing-cycle risk of owning NAND fabs. No other publication in the tracking set has weighed in, so there is no inter-source disagreement. The conviction is set at medium because the thesis is structurally sound but dependent on a specific demand driver (video-model NVMe) that was still early-stage at time of publication.

## Outcome tracking
No price at call was recorded, so quantitative tracking is not possible. The thesis would be strengthened by evidence that hyperscalers or AI labs are meaningfully growing NVMe SSD procurement for training workloads and that SIMO is retaining or gaining controller share. It would be falsified if SIMO loses key NAND OEM design wins to captive or competing merchant controllers, or if video-model training shifts to architectures that do not rely on high-throughput NVMe storage.
