## Thesis evolution
IrrationalAnalysis opened constructively in Dec 2024, viewing GFS as a credible 300mm photonics co-leader positioned for the PIC/CPO ramp. The view soured sharply through 2025 as deeper technical due diligence — including informal surveys of 40+ process engineers — revealed the Fusion node's 45nm electronics layer to be fundamentally inadequate for co-packaged optics, with chronic thermal/electrical crosstalk, poor yield, and slow cycle times. By mid-2025 GFS had become a preferred short against Tower (TSEM), framed as a valuation mis-pricing: a structurally impaired fab trading at the same P/B multiple as arguably better-positioned peers. The short thesis compounded with macro concerns (QCOM RFFE exposure heading into a mobile downcycle) and competitive threats (TSMC packaging lock-in crowding out GFS photonics wins). A narrow long signal emerged in May 2026 via GaN supply to Navitas as Innoscience faces export headwinds, but this is a single vertical and does not alter the dominant bear view. SemiAnalysis and SemiDoped coverage is lighter and more neutral, focused on Mubadala's strategic relevance and lithography optionality rather than initiating a directional stance.
