## Thesis evolution
Citrini initiated a long on ASX in July 2024 as part of a broader "picks and shovels" basket for the on-device AI inference theme. The core logic is structural: as edge AI chips pack more compute into smaller form factors (to enable local inference on iPhones and AI smartphones), advanced packaging becomes a bottleneck and a value-add. ASX (ASE Technology) is one of the world's largest OSAT providers and a direct beneficiary of rising packaging complexity. The call sits alongside AMKR as a paired advanced packaging play within the same basket, with no publication expressing a contrary view at the time of the call.

## Outcome tracking
No entry price was recorded at the time of the call, so return attribution is not possible. The thesis is contingent on the AI smartphone replacement cycle materializing — specifically the iPhone 16/17 driving a DRAM and advanced packaging demand surge. If AI smartphone unit volumes disappoint (Citrini's own cited risk) or if the complexity uplift in packaging proves more incremental than structural, the thesis weakens. The live view would be falsified if advanced packaging ASPs stagnate or if ASE loses share to rival OSATs despite rising AI chip volumes.
