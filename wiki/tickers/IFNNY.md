## Thesis evolution
The bearish view on IFNNY originates from SemiAnalysis's March 2025 analysis of the structural shift in China's automotive and industrial semiconductor supply chain. The core argument is that Chinese OEMs are actively swapping out Infineon power semiconductors in favor of domestic alternatives, driven by localization mandates and the maturation of Chinese power semis players. SemiAnalysis is the sole publication on record here, and their stance is unambiguously bearish — the loss of Chinese design wins is treated not as a cyclical risk but as a durable, accelerating structural headwind. No bullish counterpoint is captured in the call log to date.

## Outcome tracking
No price-at-call was recorded for the March 2025 short thesis, making quantitative P&L tracking impossible at this time. The thesis rests on a structural claim — Chinese OEM design-win attrition — so the key falsifier would be evidence that Infineon is winning new Chinese platform designs or that localization momentum has stalled. Confirmation would come from continued revenue pressure in Infineon's automotive segment attributable to China share loss, or public announcements from Chinese OEMs naming domestic power semiconductor suppliers in new platforms. Until revenue segmentation data or design-win disclosures emerge, the thesis remains directionally live but unverified.
