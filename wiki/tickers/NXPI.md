## Thesis evolution
NXP entered coverage in early 2024 as collateral damage from the Mobileye auto semi inventory unwind — irrationalanalysis treated it as cyclically impaired but not company-specific trouble, a "flu" rather than a seizure. By March 2025 the thesis shifted sharply negative on structural grounds: semianalysis identified NXPI as the single most exposed Western MCU supplier to Chinese localization in industrial and robotics end markets, with Gigadevice and Geehy Semi actively taking design wins. By May 2026 citrini reframed NXP entirely through the supply-chain-inheritance lens: heavy capex accumulated during the EV boom left NXP with stranded capacity that is now exactly the infrastructure AI data center power delivery demands, and the post-trauma discipline of restraining new supply additions enables ASP expansion — a structurally bullish setup that partially offsets the China displacement risk in industrial. The two live views (semianalysis bearish on MCU/China exposure, citrini bullish on analog/power AI tailwind) are in direct tension, making the net stance mixed.
