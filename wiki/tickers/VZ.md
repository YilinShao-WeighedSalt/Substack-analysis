## Thesis evolution
Coverage of VZ began as a peripheral mention: Citrini flagged all three major US telcos as marginal beneficiaries of the AI smartphone replacement cycle in July 2024, with no differentiated thesis for Verizon specifically. irrationalanalysis then floated a more idiosyncratic angle in September 2024 — that the Frontier fiber acquisition may have been motivated by hyperscaler demand for fiber to connect geographically distributed AI training clusters, a speculative read on the geographic overlap between Frontier's footprint and emerging data center corridors in Texas and the Midwest. By August 2025 that speculation had crystallized into an active LONG, with irrationalanalysis framing VZ as a defensive holding with an under-appreciated AI upside optionality from Frontier. A further wrinkle emerged in the September 2025 QCOM bear piece: Verizon's C-band rollout (targeting 80-90% completion in 2025) is the technical gating factor that determines when Apple can ship 100% internal-modem iPhones — giving VZ an inadvertent role in the Apple modem transition timeline. The only voice with a constructive stance throughout is irrationalanalysis; no publication has expressed a bearish view on VZ in this log.
