## Thesis evolution
The sole call on record is bearish, originating from irrationalanalysis in May 2025. The thesis centers on a technical execution failure: Alchip could not successfully integrate Synopsys's 200G SerDes IP into AWS Trainium 3, triggering a costly re-spin that delays tape-out and burns customer goodwill. Marvell is identified as the direct beneficiary, positioned to capture ASIC design-win share that Alchip forfeits. No bullish counterpoint exists in the tracked publications; the view is uniformly negative based on this single design failure as a signal of deeper integration risk.

## Outcome tracking
No px@call value was recorded at the time of the call, so quantitative tracking is not possible. The thesis would be falsified if Alchip publicly confirms a successful Trainium 3 tape-out without a re-spin, or if AWS signals continued engagement with Alchip on next-generation silicon. Conversely, the bear case is reinforced if Marvell announces an expanded AWS ASIC engagement or if Alchip's quarterly commentary reveals program delays or customer concentration headwinds tied to the Trainium program.
