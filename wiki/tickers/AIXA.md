## Thesis evolution
The May 2026 article extends the bull case previously built around Aixtron's InP/optics MOCVD dominance (documented in the AIXA.DE dossier) by adding a second, independently compelling growth vector: Aixtron's near-monopoly position in GaN epitaxy equipment. The author's deep technical review of GaN power semiconductors concluded that TI and Navitas hold the best GaN devices, that GaN demand for datacenter and grid power applications is set to vastly outstrip supply by H2 2027, and that equipment makers enabling GaN wafer production are the upstream beneficiary. Aixtron was singled out as the semicap play on this theme, with the author disclosing he was actively deploying fresh capital into the name alongside Infineon. Only irrationalanalysis covers AIXA in this wiki; the view has been consistently and emphatically bullish across all publication dates, with conviction deepening as additional end-markets validate the thesis.
