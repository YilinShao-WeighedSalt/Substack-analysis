## Thesis evolution
The single publication on record, irrationalanalysis, initiated a bearish view on PSTG following the OCP Global Summit in October 2025. The core concern is that Meta — a hyperscale customer that moves markets for storage vendors — is reportedly engineering around Pure Storage's solutions in favor of commodity QLC flash deployments. This is a demand-destruction thesis rather than a product-quality or balance-sheet thesis: if the largest AI infrastructure buyers can replicate Pure's value proposition with cheaper open components, Pure's premium pricing and vendor lock-in erode. No bullish counterweight from any publication exists in the call log at this time.
