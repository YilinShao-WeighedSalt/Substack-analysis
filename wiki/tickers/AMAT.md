## Thesis evolution
The AMAT bull case was established early in 2024 by both publications on overlapping but distinct grounds: semianalysis emphasized sole-source tool bundles — all-tungsten interconnect, Sculpta directional etch — that position AMAT as a structural beneficiary of each successive node transition, while irrationalanalysis framed AMAT as the indispensable infrastructure play on the semicap upcycle, holding it as its largest WFE position through mid-2024. The two camps share a long directional view but have diverged in conviction over time. Semianalysis has remained constructively engaged, consistently citing AMAT's process tools as critical to leading-edge inflections (BSPDN, High-NA EUV complementarity, TSMC N2 gate contacts), though its mentions increasingly carry nuance — the tungsten-to-molybdenum wordline transition in NAND is a concrete share-loss risk cited in January 2026, and the China export-control resilience thesis appears more conditional than previously assumed. Irrationalanalysis turned cautious by mid-2025, explicitly avoiding AMAT on the grounds that ICAPS China headwinds overwhelm the gate-all-around opportunity, a reversal from its prior largest-position framing. The result is a stock where semianalysis sees durable process-tool franchise value at each node but is surfacing fresh competitive threats, while irrationalanalysis has downgraded conviction from high to neutral on macro/regulatory headwinds.
