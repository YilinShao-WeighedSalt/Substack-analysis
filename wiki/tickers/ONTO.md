## Thesis evolution
The irrationalanalysis bull case for ONTO developed in parallel with the 2024 WFE upcycle thesis: ONTO was held alongside AMAT and CAMT as a direct beneficiary of HBM production ramp and advanced packaging inspection demand, with the position built through mid-2024 and maintained into early 2025. The first crack appeared in November 2024, when the author flagged near-term softness driven by SK Hynix and Micron holding back HBM-related WFE orders for fear Samsung had fixed its HBM3E yield problems — a game of chicken rather than a fundamental thesis break. The thesis appeared to stabilize through Q1 2025, as ONTO's own earnings commentary was cited as confirmation that Samsung was still stalling and the HBM layers race remained intact. The view reversed sharply in May 2025: irrationalanalysis concluded ONTO had failed CoWoS-L qualification at TSMC, that KLA had re-purposed its process-control tools to take advanced packaging inspection share, and that ONTO's resulting strategic pivot into process control put it on a collision course with the dominant incumbent — a structurally disadvantaged competitive position. ONTO was placed on the short side in the long KLAC / short ONTO and long CAMT / short ONTO pair trades. By December 2025 the short had been covered and ONTO re-entered as a long, though explicitly ranked below CAMT (which benefits from unrestricted China sales as an Israeli firm). The January 2026 re-buy was grounded in a new, narrower thesis — correlation to the Samsung HBM4 ramp — rather than a restoration of the prior broad-based advanced packaging inspection franchise thesis. The semianalysis mention from January 2024 was a risk flag (CXMT export restriction exposure) rather than an investment view, and semianalysis has no subsequent coverage in the log.
