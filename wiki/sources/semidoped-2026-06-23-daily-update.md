## Summary
Four key items: (1) MediaTek reportedly secured primary Google TPU v9 orders (codename Triggerfish) using 336G SerDes; Broadcom's 448G SerDes delayed. MediaTek AI ASIC revenue projected at $2B in 2026. (2) Micron and Anthropic signed strategic partnership — joint memory architecture design, long-term supply deal, and Micron investing in Anthropic's funding round. Vik noted AI companies now securing semiconductor supply chains like automakers once locked in steel. (3) US Commerce Dept accused ASML of shipping EUV-compatible components to China; ASML denied; stock fell 6.5%. (4) Samsung announced first UFS 5.0 mobile storage for on-device AI. Also covered: Jefferies forecasts memory prices +40–50% QoQ in Q3 2026 and +30–40% in Q4; cloud providers securing 50% of total memory capacity via 2-year agreements with 40% prepayment.
