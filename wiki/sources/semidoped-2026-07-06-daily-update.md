# Semi Doped Daily Update — July 6, 2026

News roundup by Austin Lyons & Vik Sekar, ~1,200 words.

## Items
- **Nvidia Kyber NVL144 delayed 12+ months to 2028** (source: SemiAnalysis). Demoed by Jensen at GTC 3 months prior; the related **NVL72x2 back-to-back rack was cancelled**, limiting **Rubin Ultra's scale-up domain**. Semi Doped reads it as favorable for [[AMD]] (Helios/MI455X ships 2H26, MI500 2027; Advancing AI event in 2 weeks). Vik had floated this on X a month prior.
- **Nvidia revenue-share financing** (source: The Information) — same story as the SemiAnalysis backstop piece. Vik: recurring revenue smooths NVDA financials vs lumpy POs, analogizes to Qualcomm's decades-long CDMA royalty cut. Austin: financing is an underrated moat vs accelerator startups.
- **$510B global AI startup investment H1 2026** (Crunchbase); OpenAI + Anthropic captured **43% of total VC**; US hyperscaler AI capex projected **3.2% of GDP by 2027**; 16 companies raised $1B+ rounds in Q2.
- **Intel hikes CPU prices** (Tom's Hardware) — enthusiast chips +$30–50; some datacenter Xeon 8000 'Emerald Rapids' now priced above their late-2023 launch (increases of hundreds–thousands). Vik: pricing power = good for INTC financials (AMD forecasts high demand to 2030/31); Austin: inflation after memory.
- **Hon Hai (Foxconn, [[2317.TW]]) Q2 beat** on **record AI-server revenue** (Bloomberg/Reuters) — AI servers now exceed consumer electronics; Oracle capex cited as demand driver; "no slowdown even as there are fears of overcapacity."
- **South Korea growth fund** seeded by chip-tax revenue (Yonhap) — Samsung + SK Hynix committed **≥₩1,350T (~$880B)** on chips/data centers; Seoul wants to recycle the memory windfall.
- **Tweet — Intel "XBM"** (Cross-Batch Memory) patent published: ultra-high-bandwidth memory, potential HBM4 competitor ("don't let it be Optane 2.0").
- **Tweet — interconnects > memory** as the binding AI constraint (Big Boss / outside five sigma).

## Calls
- [[NVDA]] — **NEUTRAL/MIXED** @ $195.84 — Kyber NVL144 delay (>12mo to 2028) + NVL72x2 cancellation are bearish product datapoints; offset by the bullish revenue-share financing moat.
- [[AMD]] — **LONG** @ $552.05 — Kyber delay opens a window; Helios (MI455X) 2H26 + MI500 2027.
- [[INTC]] — **LONG** @ $120.94 — CPU price hikes signal pricing power in a supply-tight market; XBM patent = optional HBM4 competitor.
- [[2317.TW]] — **LONG** @ ~NT$255 — record AI-server revenue, Q2 beat; clean proxy for compute deployment, no slowdown.
- [[005930.KS]] / [[000660.KS]] — **MENTION** — Korea chip-tax growth fund; joint ≥$880B capex commitment (policy/context, no directional call).
