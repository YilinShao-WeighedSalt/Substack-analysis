# The Future of Meta Superintelligence: A 1 Year Progress Update

Authors: Max Kan, Julien Martin-Prin, Jeremie Eliahou Ontiveros, Dylan Patel. One year after the Llama 4 flop pushed Zuck to rebuild Meta's AI org (MSL), a progress check. First public model "Muse Spark" (April) lagged DeepSeek V4-Pro and Kimi K2.6 — but "what matters is the slope, not the intercept." Thesis: **Meta is the only company on track to be world-class at all three frontier-model ingredients — data, talent, compute — and has the best shot at catching Anthropic/OpenAI.** Google has "faded"; Microsoft blew its OpenAI-IP lead; Chinese labs are compute-poor.

**Data (newest, most underappreciated edge):** RL is today's dominant scaling law — teach the model to complete whole tasks in an *environment* with *tools* + *verifiers* (rubrics). A human-data supply chain (Mercor, Surge, Handshake at $1B+ ARR; Fleet/Mechanize/Afterquery ~$100M) sprang up. The prize: **real screen recordings of white-collar work** (realistic by definition; also seed rubrics + deterministic verifiers). Meta started **tracking employees' screens/keyboard/mouse** — "some of the most valuable data in the world" — and reassigned **~3,000 engineers (incl. 70% of new grads) to make RL tasks full-time**. Effectively spawned a top-tier RL-environment company in-house; Mercor logged 2.517M expert-hours in 2Q26 (~4,800 FTE) — Meta already in the same ballpark with ~70k more to pull from. Top data-task contractors make 7 figures; labs pay $5,000+ per good coding task.

**Compute:** hyperscaler balance sheet, no cloud-rental drag, Zuck willing to run FCF-negative → Tokenomics Model projects **more AI compute than OpenAI or Anthropic by end-2026**. Building **five 1GW+ "titan" clusters simultaneously**: Prometheus (Ohio, partially live, expanding ~1GW→>3GW), Hyperion (Louisiana, 3×400MW — world's largest single buildings), plus El Paso, Iowa (nothing→full GW in 1 yr), Indiana. "Never in history" two full-GW campuses under construction at once — Meta has that now.

**Scale-across (networking):** Prometheus = 27 datacenters across 6 campuses (5 within 6km, 6th ~75–80km). "AI-Backbone (AIBB)": L3 Superspines interconnecting DSF/NSF scale-out regions, aggregated on an L4 Inter-BAG hub, ~22 Pbit/s bidirectional. Intra-region latency 1–10µs; 100km apart ≥500µs (light-speed limit) → forces asynchronous training (pretrain synchronous in 1 region; RL spread globally). Future titans connect campuses up to **2,000km apart**. Uses LR/ZR optics + DWDM.

**Talent:** ex-OpenAI/Anthropic/Google + Thinking Machines cofounders (Andrew Tulloch et al.); Dina Powell McCormick as President/Vice-Chair for compute buildout; poached OpenAI's compute team (1 already quit over infra-org culture).

**Bull but "step 1":** falsifiers SA names — a long-term no-clawback compute-sale deal, disbanding the RL-task org, or losing top researchers = "materially less bullish"/"death sentence." Muse Spark 1.1 ≈ Opus 4.6 / GLM 5.2 for agentic use; SA won't move its own token volume onto it yet; doesn't expect parity with Anthropic/OpenAI before end-2026. (Google implications paywalled.)
