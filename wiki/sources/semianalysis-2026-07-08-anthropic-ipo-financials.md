# Anthropic 3Q26 Profit Over $1B: The Anthropic IPO Financials Sneak Peek

Authors: Joey Brookhart, Crystal Huang, Dylan Patel. (Financial deep-dive paywalled.) Anthropic (founded 2021 by Dario Amodei ex-OpenAI; Claude + Claude Code) **confidentially filed for IPO June 1**. No public numbers → SA's Tokenomics team reconstructs financials bottom-up by SKU/tier/customer (a recent WSJ piece reportedly confirmed the method's accuracy).

Headline claims: **Anthropic 3Q26 profit >$1B** (notable since AI labs usually burn cash); Anthropic + OpenAI ≈ **$100B combined ARR**; Anthropic is the **clear leader in profitable B2B AI** vs an "unfocused and money-burning" OpenAI. With that lead, Anthropic can reinvest superior margins into better models. SA floats **$6T valuation as a base-case possibility** if execution continues, and frames the IPO as putting pressure on OpenAI (reportedly delaying its own IPO to 2027) to open books and raise capital. Two Chinese labs (Zhipu, Minimax) already IPO'd in 2026; Anthropic would be the first Western lab of this scale.

Notes: SA is culturally close to Anthropic (Meta post same week notes an Anthropic researcher is the author's roommate) → treat the pro-Anthropic/anti-OpenAI tilt and the modeled numbers as SA's construction, not disclosure.
