## Summary
Non-semiconductor primary post. Argues "attention" is the scarcest market resource — every analyst is focused on AI infrastructure, creating an "attention tax" where other sectors are systematically under-researched. Five non-semi themes behind paywall: senior housing, live entertainment, exchange competition, fintech resurgence, airlines. Macro view: market goes higher but with more frequent 10–15% drawdowns from positioning rather than fundamentals. Recommends keeping semi names but diversifying. Citrini has been trimming AI allocation. The "AI exhaustion" framing is a portfolio-construction observation, not a bearish semiconductor call.
