# Semi Doped Daily Update — July 8, 2026

News roundup by Austin Lyons & Vik Sekar.

## Items
- **Apple ([[AAPL]]) tests Chinese CXMT DRAM** for China-market devices (iPhones etc.) — confined to China to dodge US export controls; Lexar DDR5-7600 also using CXMT. CXMT is #4 memory maker behind the big-3; output largely pre-committed (Ray Wang/SA quoted in FT). Chinese memory gaining consumer traction.
- **FuriosaAI + Equinix** — Korean inference-chip startup deploys accelerators across Equinix's European colocation; targets Nvidia alternatives / sovereign/TCO-sensitive deployments.
- **Amazon ([[AMZN]]) raises $25B in bonds** — one of the largest US corporate debt sales ever, 3–40yr maturities, funding ~$100B+ AI-capex plan. (Macro recap: demand reportedly soft.)
- **Wolfspeed ([[WOLF]]) sues Navitas ([[NVTS]])** — SiC (and per the patent list, GaN too) infringement across "the entire Navitas portfolio"; seeks damages + sales injunction. Broad legal overhang for Navitas.
- **Samsung begins NVMe mass production for Nvidia Vera Rubin** — storage/memory into the Blackwell successor platform.
- **Zhipu AI (z.ai) weighs custom chip** — preliminary inquiries to Chinese design houses; demand + export controls. Custom-silicon wave: "the hardware and model layer is coalescing."
- **DeepSeek develops custom AI chip** (Reuters) — separate from interim Huawei Ascend use; no timeline/partner. Austin: "bearish Huawei, not Nvidia."
- **Samsung delays CXL 3.1 memory (CMM-D 3.0)** — waiting on CXL-3.1 CPUs from Intel (Diamond Rapids) and AMD (Venice); keeps producing CXL 2.0. Full production now ~next year.
- **Synopsys ([[SNPS]]) exits fab-manufacturing software** (EES, Fault Detection & Classification) to refocus on AI-driven chip-design tools.
- **Analog Devices ([[ADI]]) closes Empower Semiconductor** — integrated voltage regulators / vertical power delivery; "extends power portfolio from grid to core."
- Quick hits: NAND prices surging toward DRAM; China smartphone sales −13% at 618 festival (memory-cost squeeze); Syntiant (Intel-backed edge-AI) files US IPO; Penguin Solutions record $479M sales +48% YoY.
