# Semi Doped Daily Update — July 9, 2026

News roundup by Austin Lyons & Vik Sekar.

## Items
- **China clears AI firms to buy Nvidia ([[NVDA]]) H200** (The Information) — policy shift; H200 (HBM GPU, below H100 in restriction tiers). Vik: maybe "too little, too late" (China building domestic compute; GLM 5.2 cyber worries); Austin: momentum shifts back to Nvidia, easier to buy the best. Jensen got what he wanted (keep China "hooked on American accelerators").
- **SK Hynix ([[000660.KS]]) $28B ADR bookbuild oversubscribed**, closes Wednesday. Panel caution: Cerebras/SpaceX IPOs also oversubscribed then disappointed.
- **Nvidia + d-Matrix joint AI system** — integrating hardware; d-Matrix (inference startup, last $2B val, raising again). Pattern of Nvidia partnering with inference challengers as a hedge; likely heterogeneous compute (GPU prefill + d-Matrix decode).
- **SambaNova raises $1B Series F at $11B** (General Atlantic led; Intel a backer); **JPMorganChase signs as customer** for on-prem inference. Trend: enterprises deploying own compute to keep data off AI-lab clouds.
- **APS acquires UPI (18B won)** — Korean transformer-automation leader (95% cut-to-length machine share); entry into AI-datacenter power infrastructure (765kV UHV transformers, grid replacement).
- **ZML (French) releases ZML/LLMD** — hardware-agnostic LLM inference server across Nvidia/AMD/Google/Apple/Intel; breaks vendor lock-in (à la Qualcomm's Modular acquisition).
- **Apple–Broadcom ([[AAPL]]/[[AVGO]]) finalize $30B deal** — RF components + FBAR filters (NOT AI chips); Broadcom $1.5B Fort Collins CO expansion under Apple's American Manufacturing Program. Reads partly as "made in USA" publicity.
- **Bloom Energy ([[BE]]) — Hunterbrook short report:** alleges Bloom's fuel cells depend on Chinese **scandium** requiring per-shipment CCP approval ("China has an OFF switch to US datacenters"); CEO repeatedly denied a China supply chain. Caveats: Hunterbrook is short BE; Bloom says cells "supplement grid power"; most target datacenters not built yet.
- Sector: **Besi ([[BESI]])** hybrid-bonding "blue ocean" now contested; OpenAI GPT-5.6 family public launch Thursday post-clearance; Blue Owl new data-center infra venture; **Microsoft lays off ~4,800**; **Eaton ([[ETN]])** names Dan Simpson President of Global Energy Infrastructure Solutions; Arteris + imec IC-Link NoC collaboration.
