# Meta Compute: Everyone Wants To Be A Neocloud

SemiAnalysis's rebuttal to the Bloomberg-driven sell-off (Jul 1–2) that dumped neocloud stocks [[CRWV]] and [[NBIS]] on "Meta becomes a neocloud / overcapacity" fears. SA argues **both the panic and the overcapacity read are erroneous** and that [[META]]'s compute procurement will *accelerate*, not slow.

Key points:
- Meta contracted **>5 GW of capacity across cloud & colo in 1H 2026 alone**, excluding self-build; two largest campuses = 2.5 GW under construction. Cross-references SA's "Stop Saying Half of US Datacenters Are Delayed."
- Four high-value uses for the compute keep buying rational: (1) frontier training in **Meta Superintelligence Labs (MSL)**; (2) **RecSys** — ad recommendation AI Meta thinks it can scale >10× in complexity to accelerate revenue; (3) **Bedrock-type** token-as-a-service — SA exclusive: *Meta in final talks with Anthropic for private Claude instances*; (4) **"SpaceX-type"** premium on-demand compute deals.
- SpaceX reportedly priced Anthropic/Google compute deals at **3–4× peer rates** (~$50B/GW annual revenue); the Google deal is effectively a 3-month rolling contract (3yr with 90-day mutual cancel). Only **Oracle and Meta** can realistically match the financing.
- Oracle framed as having **under-monetized its gigawatts** vs SpaceX — "a massive blow."
- Read-through: Meta will be a **huge source of RPO growth for CoreWeave, Nebius and others** (bulk of Meta's additions now via 3rd parties) — opposite of the sell-off logic.
- Meta's "tent" ultra-fast datacenter design supports fast, monetizable capacity.
