[[citrini]] presents a thesis on telecom optical networking as the next AI infrastructure bottleneck, arguing that chronic underinvestment in network capacity has created a generational entry point as AI-driven demand triggers a new capex upcycle. The subtitle is "The Coming Optics Shortage & Telecom Suppliers' New Dawn."

## The Core Setup: Cyclical Trough Meets Secular Inflection

The piece's central framework is the convergence of two forces: a temporary cyclical depression in telecom capex (inventory right-sizing, carriers pulling back) colliding with a structural secular demand inflection driven by AI data center buildout. Citrini argues that the most lucrative equity opportunities historically arise precisely at this intersection — where a name is out-of-favor on cyclical grounds just as a durable secular tailwind is beginning.

The historical template invoked is Nvidia (NVDA), which hit its cyclical trough in August 2022 amid $1.32 billion in inventory write-downs and a $1.4 billion revenue miss, only for that trough to coincide with the secular AI wave. NVDA subsequently gained over 1,200% from trough to peak. A second analogy is Eaton Corporation (ETN), which Citrini had previously flagged as an "archetypal fiscal beneficiary" when consensus was pessimistic on cyclical industrials — ahead of government infrastructure spending that drove substantial equity appreciation.

## The Structural Demand Case

AI data centers are not just growing in number; each is becoming more compute-dense and requiring inter-cluster and inter-data-center connectivity at scale. The result is an exponential increase in network nodes — from hyperscaler campuses to smartphones, AR/VR headsets, surgical robots, and industrial automation — each demanding more bandwidth. Citrini argues that fiber optic span capacity has failed to keep pace with this explosion in nodes, and carriers have materially underinvested even relative to current (not future) demand. This creates a supply gap that must close through a sustained network upgrade cycle.

## The Primary Equity Call

Ciena (CIEN) is identified as the primary expression of the thesis: an optical networking equipment supplier that is cyclically depressed but positioned to be a direct beneficiary as telco and hyperscaler capex recovers and accelerates. Citrini frames CIEN as occupying the same setup that NVDA did in mid-2022 — entering a generational upgrade cycle from a washed-out valuation base. The post characterizes the timing as a "bases loaded" moment for the trade.
