## Summary
Key items: (1) Intel shares rose ~9% after President Trump announced Apple agreed to design and manufacture chips in the US with Intel, shifting significant volume from TSMC. Semi Doped called this continued validation of Intel's foundry strategy. (2) Amazon plans to sell Trainium AI chips directly to datacenter operators — no longer AWS-exclusive. Google made similar move in April with TPUs. Both now compete directly with Nvidia in AI chip hardware sales. (3) Samsung leveraging excess fab capacity to attract AMD as advanced-node customer.
