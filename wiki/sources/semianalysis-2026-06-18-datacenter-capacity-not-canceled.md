[[semianalysis]] refutes the claim that half of 2026 US datacenter capacity has been delayed or canceled. Their datacenter tracking model shows YE2026 NA Hyperscaler self-build forecast moved by only ~1% over 6 months. The "50% delayed" figure from Bloomberg/Sightline Climate is based on a flawed denominator (top two hyperscalers alone exceed 5GW under construction). Detailed case studies of Nebius NJ (delays but 50MW delivered by Feb 2026), Core Scientific Denton (weather/transformer delays), and Oracle/STACK NM (delayed to 2029 due to gas pipeline/permitting issues). Equipment supplier backlogs are secure because canceled projects never placed orders; prepayments lock queue positions. Chinese OEMs quietly alleviating bottlenecks. Modular/prefab builds compressing timelines.
