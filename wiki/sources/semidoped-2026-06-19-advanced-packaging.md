## Summary
Educational deep-dive podcast on advanced packaging, building from first principles (wire bonding, flip chip, OSATs) to investment-relevant conclusions. Explains CoWoS variants (CoWoS-S, CoWoS-R, CoWoS-L) vs Intel EMIB, covering EMIB's panel-based scalability advantage and the 90% yield debate (hosts argue Intel's decade of EMIB shipping history contradicts the bear case). Covers EMIB-T (through-silicon vias) and EMIB-M (embedded capacitors). Key revelation: Google has reportedly committed to ~3 million TPUs packaged using Intel EMIB, targeted ~2028, designed with MediaTek rather than Broadcom — a double disruption of TSMC CoWoS and Broadcom's custom ASIC monopoly. SK Hynix reportedly testing EMIB for HBM integration. Scaling roadmap: EMIB at ~8x reticle today targeting 12x+ by 2028 vs TSMC CoWoS-L at ~5.5x targeting 9.5x.
