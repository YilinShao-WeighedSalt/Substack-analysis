# Semi Doped Daily Update — July 7, 2026

News roundup by Austin Lyons & Vik Sekar.

- **Nvidia denies Kyber AI-server delay** — refuted the SemiAnalysis 1-yr-delay report; spokesperson says roadmap "remains intact"; Kyber (144-GPU vertical rack) launches with Vera Rubin Ultra 2H27. Vik: an off-cycle social-media rebuttal isn't how "the big leagues play."

- **SK Hynix ([[000660.KS]]) formally launches $28–29B US ADR** — seeking valuation premium vs under-valued Seoul listing; Coatue + Situational Awareness indicated up to $7B combined (Tae Kim reading the filing).
