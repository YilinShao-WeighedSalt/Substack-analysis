[[citrini]] presents a new single-stock long thesis (subtitled "Inspector Data Center") centered on a "relatively undiscovered" company with exposure to data centers, grid reliability, and automation. The actual featured company is behind the paywall; the publicly available introduction establishes the investment framework and situates the pick within Citrini's evolving AI infrastructure thematic.

## Investment Framework

The post traces Citrini's progression through the AI infrastructure buildout in three phases. In 2023, the focus was on GPU server supply — names like Nvidia, Supermicro, and Celestica (CLS) — when the primary bottleneck was compute. Next came the connectivity layer inside and between data centers, with Credo Semiconductor (CRDO) and Ciena as the key ideas. Most recently, Citrini moved up the stack to high-voltage power management, featuring American Superconductor (AMSC) and Siemens Energy as the grid-scale enablers of large-scale data center operations.

## The Next Phase: Greenfield Infrastructure

The thesis argues that the AI buildout has now progressed beyond retrofitting existing facilities. At the scale of several trillion dollars over five years, the capital is going into greenfield data center construction and all the foundational infrastructure required to support it — spanning resource consumption, maintenance, and utility-grade systems. This convergence of AI capex with other megatrends (fiscal primacy, robotics/embodied AI) creates investment opportunities well outside the traditional chip-and-server value chain.

## The Featured Long

The specific stock is paywalled. Citrini describes it as a name with "significant upside to trends in data centers, grid reliability and automation" trading at "a multiple reflective of a lack of understanding by the market" — a valuation discount attributable to low investor awareness rather than fundamental weakness. The thesis hinges on the convergence of fiscal priorities, technological innovation, and AI capex spending. The subtitle "Inspector Data Center" is offered as a thematic codename for the pick.

## Historical Track Record as Context

Citrini draws a straight line through its prior single-stock calls to establish credibility: CLS (Celestica) and CRDO (Credo Semiconductor) as early AI infrastructure winners; AMSC (American Superconductor) as the power-layer beneficiary. Each prior pick corresponded to a distinct phase of the buildout, and the new pick is framed as the natural next step in that sequence.
